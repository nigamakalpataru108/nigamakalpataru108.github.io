# Module 4: Diksa Preparation

1. Bhagavad Gita as it is
2. Srimad Bhagavatam Canto 1
3. Bhakti Rasamrta Sindhu
4. Life and Teachings of Lord Chaitanya
5. Srila Prabhupada Biography
